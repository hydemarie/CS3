About Us

This is a voluntary work.

Long-term goals include assisting hundreds of thousands of children in the Philippines - providing them the chance to achieve their potential.

We guide parents in understanding their roles and responsibilities to children and assist them in positively and respectfully engaging and empowering way.

We safeguard everyone affiliated with our organization from all forms of harm.

We protect the rights of children.

Who can volunteer?

Anyone between 18 to 60 years old. They can come from all different backgrounds and bring diverse skill that we can further develop.
We value the support of every single one of our volunteers.

Volunteering is a great opportunity to make new friends and join a group of devoted supporters. Additionally, you have the chance to enhance your professional possibilities and skill set. Of course, you'll be playing a crucial part in aiding in the protection of young lives.

You not only help kids when you volunteer ; there are also big benefits for you.

VISION

Our vision is a world in which every child attains quality education for a quality future ahead of them.

MISSION

To achieve a lasting change in children's lives through the power of education.
