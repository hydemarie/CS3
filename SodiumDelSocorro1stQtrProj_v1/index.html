Action in Education

the Philippines

The country's financial position is one of the top reasons adding to the expanding schooling troubles in the Philippines, and this was true even before the COVID-19 pandemic occurred and caused problems for millions of families. In addition, more kids, teens, and adults are left behind because they don't have equal access to educational opportunities.

In the future, these problems can have much more severe consequences. The current situation and the ways in which we can participate in social activities to combat these important challenges facing our country will now be examined in detail.
